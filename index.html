HR report using Power BI

In this dashboard, we cleaned the data and then created different measures using DAX.
We finally got a report that has different insights within the organization:
- The total number of employees and how many of them are females and males.
- Service years for the employees.
- Distance status from work in order to understand the employees situations.
- The number and percentage of employees who are qualified for promotion and aren't qualified yet.
- The number and percentage of employees who are on service and the employees that unfortunately will be letting go.

Taking Airbnb dataset from 2016. Started by cleaning the data in order to reach the goal which is helping a potential buyer planning to buy a house in an area where Airbnb businesses are working very well.
